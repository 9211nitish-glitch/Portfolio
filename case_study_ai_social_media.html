The Challenge

Managing constant social media presence across numerous platforms requires immense creative energy and strict execution routines. For event organizers and businesses, building consistent content calendars, maintaining brand tone, and writing copies manual-by-manual became bottleneck points.

The goal was to design a platform where users could feed baseline campaign goals, and an AI agent would generate multi-format image briefs, caption sets, hashtags, and schedule them across multiple social channels automatically with minimal friction.

Manual Friction

Writing unique copies for Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram takes hours weekly.

Publishing Inconsistencies

Missing peak engagement hours due to time delays and scheduling fatigue.
