BTN Events CRM Portal

The Challenge

Event management companies deal with massive influxes of inquiries daily. Tracking the history of client conversions, event details (themes, dates, location parameters), coordinator tasks, and payments in separate spreadsheets lead to heavy administrative overload and missed follow-ups.

The goal was to design and deploy a central custom CRM that pulls data from lead channels, assigns tasks to team members automatically, logs communications, and displays detailed analytical dashboards.

Lead Leakage

Unmanaged spreadsheet leads lost due to slow response times.

Task Fragmentation

Difficulty tracking coordinator tasks and payment records in real-time.
